Bootcamp 2020: 'Tap the Ball' Game!

This is my first programming project, concluding the 2 week Programming Bootcamp at City University of London. This Processing project demonstrates a comprehensive understanding of the use of different variables and loops throughout. I created a game where you have to tap a bouncing ball, but whenever you tap the ball the speed increases, making it more challenging each time you tap. A tap increases your score in increments of one, whereas if you miss the ball, one of your three lives will be taken away from you. I have used different variables to change the speed each time the ball was tapped, using 'if or else' statements.
